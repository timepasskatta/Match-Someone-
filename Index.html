Friend Compatibility Test

Fill out the quiz and see how much you and your friend think alike!

How to Check Score:

  1. First, fill out the form yourself.
  2. Then, share the form link with your friend.
  3. Download responses from Google Forms into Google Sheets.
  4. Use a formula to check matching answers:
    =COUNTIF(A2:A101, B2:B101)/100*100
  5. The result is your match percentage!
